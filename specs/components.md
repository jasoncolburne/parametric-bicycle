# Compatible Components

## Frame Interface Summary

- **Bottom bracket**: BSA 68mm threaded
- **Rear axle**: 142×12mm boost thru-axle
- **Head tube**: 44mm ID (1-1/8" straight steerer)
- **Seatpost**: 27.2mm diameter
- **Brake mounts**: Post Mount (74mm spacing)
- **Rotor size**: 180mm front, 160mm rear
- **Wheel size**: 700c / 29"

---

## Motor & Battery

### Mid-Drive Motor

**Tongsheng TSDZ2 750W** (recommended for hills)
- Mount: BSA 68mm
- Power: 750W nominal
- Torque: 80Nm + torque-sensing
- Weight: 3.6kg
- Includes: Controller, display, PAS sensor
- Torque-sensing provides natural pedal feel - motor responds to how hard you push

Alternatives:
- Bafang BBSHD 1000W (more raw power, cadence-sensing)
- Bafang BBS02B 750W (cadence-sensing, budget)

### Battery

**52V 14Ah (728Wh)** - shark pack style, downtube mounted
- Dimensions: ~365×85×65mm
- Weight: ~3.2kg
- Range: 45-75km depending on assist level and hills
- Higher voltage = better hill performance and efficiency
- Connector: Compatible with TSDZ2 (requires 52V controller)
- **Mounting:** Water bottle cage style (74mm spacing) - uses rivnuts embedded in downtube
- See [Battery & Water Bottle Mounting Documentation](../docs/battery_water_bottle_mounting.md)

Alternatives:
- 52V 17.5Ah (910Wh) - more range for longer hilly rides
- 48V 14Ah (672Wh) - standard voltage option

**Battery Mounting Hardware (included in frame):**
- 6× M5 aluminum rivnuts in downtube section 1
- 2× CNC-milled aluminum battery brackets (74mm spacing)
- 4× Velcro straps for battery retention

## Wheels

### Front Wheel

**700c disc wheel**
- Axle: 100×12mm or 100×9mm QR
- Spoke count: 32
- Rim: Double-wall aluminum, disc-specific
- Hub: Centerlock or 6-bolt disc

### Rear Wheel

**700c disc wheel**
- Axle: 142×12mm thru-axle (boost)
- Spoke count: 32 or 36 (for cargo capacity)
- Rim: Double-wall aluminum, disc-specific
- Hub: Shimano HG freehub body

Recommended: **DT Swiss G540 rims** with quality hubs, or pre-built commuter wheelset

---

## Fork

**Rigid steel/aluminum fork** (recommended for commuter)
- Steerer: 1-1/8" straight, threadless
- Axle: 100×12mm or 100×9mm QR
- Brake: Disc mount (IS or post)
- Tire clearance: 45mm+

Options:
- Surly Midnight Special Fork (steel, robust)
- Whisky No.7 Carbon (lightweight)
- Generic aluminum rigid fork (budget)

For suspension:
- SR Suntour NCX (63mm travel, lockout)
- RockShox Paragon (65mm, 1-1/8" steerer)

## Headset

**1-1/8" integrated headset**
- Type: IS42/28.6 (integrated, straight)
- Fits 44mm head tube ID
- Sealed cartridge bearings

Options:
- Cane Creek 40 Series
- FSA Orbit

## Brakes

**Hydraulic disc brakes**
- Mount: Post Mount (direct mount, no adapters)
- Rotor: **180mm front** (better stopping on steep descents), 160mm rear

Options:
- **Shimano MT200** (budget, reliable)
- **Shimano Deore M6100** (better modulation)
- **TRP Spyre** (mechanical, easy maintenance)
- **Magura MT4** (high-end)

## Drivetrain

### Cassette

**9-speed wide-range** (essential for hills)
- **Sunrace 11-42T** (recommended for steep climbs)
- Or Shimano 11-36T if hills are moderate

### Chain

**9-speed chain**
- Shimano HG53
- KMC X9

### Rear Derailleur

**9-speed**
- Shimano Alivio RD-M3100
- Or Shimano Altus RD-M2000

### Shifter

**9-speed trigger shifter**
- Shimano Alivio SL-M3100
- Right side only (no front derailleur with mid-drive)

### Chainring

**Included with mid-drive motor**
- **42T recommended** (better climbing, lower top speed)
- 46T for balance of climbing and speed
- Narrow-wide profile recommended
